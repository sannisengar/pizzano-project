about Pinanzo

Pinanzo is a new-age consumer-first organization offering an easy-to-use convenience platform, accessible through a unified website.

THE MAN BEHIND THE Pinanzo

With over 30 restaurants in 23 years under his belt, the prolific restaurateur sanni sengar has become somewhat of a legend in the industry, starting his journey at a mere age of 19. sanni was the first in his family to venture into business, hailing from a family of lawyers, going on to create an empire in F&B. Following his heart and tongue through his F&B career, sanni has successfully created some of the most talked-about brands in the industry - Diablo, Lord of the Drinks, Plum by Bent Chair, Lazeez Affaire, Tamasha, The Flying Saucer Cafe, and many more.




Mission

Our mission is to elevate the quality of life of the urban consumer by offering unparalleled convenience. Convenience is what makes us tick. It’s what makes us get out of bed and say, “Let’s do this.”

Vision

"To revolutionize the way the world experiences food by pioneering innovative, sustainable, and nutritious solutions that delight our customers and enhance the quality of life for communities around the globe."

Values
